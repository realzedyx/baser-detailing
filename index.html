Why Baser Detailing

Not the same as a car wash.

A shopping centre car wash and a proper detail are apples and oranges. Five people spending 90 minutes on your car at volume is a rinse and vacuum — it's not the same job. Here's what's actually different.

👤

One person, start to finish

It's me on your car the entire time. No handoffs, no crew rotating through, no one rushing to clock off. You book me — I do the work.

Car washes split your car across multiple staff in a production line. Nobody owns the result.
🔍

Thorough, not fast

A full detail takes 4–5 hours. That's not inefficiency — that's what it takes to do stain removal, get into every surface properly, and hand dry the paint without swirl marks.

A 90-minute wash with five people is about throughput. They're onto the next car before yours is dry.
📍

I come to you

No driving somewhere, waiting around, then driving back. You go about your day at home and the car gets sorted in your driveway.

A shopping centre wash means losing a chunk of your day. Mobile detailing means you don't have to go anywhere.

How it runs

Inside out, in that order.

Interior first while the car's dry, wheels next as the dirtiest job, then the full exterior wash and dry. It's the order that keeps the finish clean and nothing gets re-dirtied.

01

Interior first

The full cabin gets done while everything's dry — vacuum, surfaces, glass and mats — so no overspray or runoff lands on fresh work.

02

Then the wheels

The dirtiest part of the car gets isolated — wheels, barrels, arches and tyres — before any panel washing starts.

03

Exterior to finish

Thorough wash from top to bottom, then a careful hand dry. The paint is the last thing touched and the first thing you'll notice.

Good to know

How long does it take?
Around 4–5 hours for a full detail. I only take one car a day, so yours is never rushed.
Do you come to me?
Always. Pick a time that suits you and I'll bring everything to your place — you don't need to go anywhere.
What do you need from me?
A tap and a power point nearby, and enough room to work around the car — not on a busy main road. If that's not possible where you are, I offer free pickup and drop-off, or you can bring the car to me.
What if it rains?
If the weather won't let me do the job properly, I'll message you and we'll move it to the next clear day — no charge to reschedule.
How do I pay?
A 20% deposit secures your booking. The rest is paid on the day by PayID, card or cash, once you're happy with the job.
My promise I treat every car like my own. If something's not right, tell me before I leave and I'll fix it.
